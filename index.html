Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich, creamy North Indian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) simmered in a buttery tomato-based gravy.

Ingredients

  • 200g Panner, cubed
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1tbsp oil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 onion, finelt chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves), crushed
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Step-By-Step Process

  1. Sauté aromatics: Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add bay leaf and chopped onions. Sauté until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste: Cook until raw smell disappears.
  3. Spice it up: Add turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and garam masala. Mix well.
  4. Tomato base: Pour in tomato puree. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Creamy touch: Add cream and kasuri methi. Stir and simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Paneer time: Add paneer cubes. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish: Top with fresh coriander and a drizzle of cream.

Masala Dosa

Crispy fermented crepes stuffed with spiced potato filling—South India’s iconic breakfast

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • ½ cup urad dal
  • 1 tbsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 boiled potatoes
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • ½ tsp mustard seeds
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • Curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil

Step-By-Step Process

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds for 6 hours.
  2. Grind to a smooth paste, add salt, and ferment overnight.
  3. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, green chilies, and onions.
  4. Sauté until golden, add turmeric and mashed potatoes.
  5. Mix well and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Heat a tawa, pour batter, spread thin. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Drizzle oil, cook until crisp.
  8. Place masala in center, fold and serve.

Lemon Rice

Zesty, golden rice tempered with mustard seeds and curry leaves—quick, tangy comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup cooked rice
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 tbsp mustard seeds
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • Curry leaves
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil

Step-By-Step Process

  1. - Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add green chilies and curry leaves. Sauté for a few seconds.
  3. - Add turmeric and salt. Mix well.
  4. Add cooked rice and mix until evenly coated.
  5. Turn off heat, squeeze lemon juice, and mix again.
  6. Garnish with roasted peanuts or cashews. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Bisi Bele Bath

Zesty, golden rice tempered with mustard seeds and curry leaves—quick, tangy comfort food.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup toor dal
  • ½ cup rice
  • 1½ tbsp bisi bele bath powder
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste
  • Mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • ½ tsp mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • 1 dried red chili
  • A pinch of hing (asafoetida)

Step-By-Step Process

  1. - Pressure cook dal and rice together until soft.
  2. Boil chopped vegetables until tender.
  3. - In a pot, mix cooked dal-rice, vegetables, tamarind paste, bisi bele bath powder, and salt.
  4. - Add water to adjust consistency. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  5. - Heat ghee/oil, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, red chili, and hing.
  6. Pour tempering over the bath and mix well.
  7. - Serve hot with boondi or potato chips.
